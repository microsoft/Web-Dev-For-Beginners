Sometimes Waste is not just Waste

Waste management refers to the various schemes to manage and dispose of wastes. It can be by discarding, destroying, processing, recycling, reusing, or controlling wastes. The prime objective of waste management is to reduce the amount of unusable materials and to avert potential health and environmental hazards. Waste Management has proven its commitment to developing innovative ways to handle solid waste and recyclables in India.With a customer service-centered approach that filters up from the bottom,and an unwavering insistence on keeping recycling and other environmental protections at the forefront of everything we do.
The easiest way to save a product from being wasted is by not letting the product become waste . This means we will try to use it in some other way once its purpose has been fulfilled. Its not always necessary to throw the products into the dustbin, we should always analyze and think if it can be used further .
There are 3 R's of managing waste and thus saving our environment -
✓REUSE- donating unused products to make sure others can reuse them too!
✓REDUCE-by buying what you need,reduce the amount of materials you consume.
✓RECYCLE -buying recycled goods is a way to participate in this part of the 3 R's .
